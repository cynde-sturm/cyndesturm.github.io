Background

Clinician, researcher, educator.

I'm an APRN and clinical research nurse based in Houston, TX. My career spans ICU nursing at MD Anderson Cancer Center, 8+ years as a primary study coordinator in a high-volume academic research unit, and graduate-level work in nursing education. I've served as the primary clinical contact for complex patients across oncology, hematology, cardiology, neurology, rare disease, infectious disease, and gene therapy — including viral vector studies designed to alter DNA expression and enable enzyme production.

My research experience includes cardiovascular device trials, gene therapy, oncology, rare disease, and decentralized studies — spanning Phase 1 through Phase 4 across both academic and remote settings. My current role at Scripps Research Translational Institute supports a national decentralized Long COVID trial remotely.
